Investing in Opportunity Zone funds provides tax advantages:

  • The original gain is deferred and recognized on December 31, 2026
  • No federal tax due at exit if investment is held for more than 10 years
  • No recapture of depreciation and refinance proceeds upon exit

A hypothetical scenario compares OZ & non-OZ investments to illustrate the tax implications which may lead to a tax advantage of ~400 bps.
